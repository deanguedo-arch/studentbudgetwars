# Classic Consequence Depth Master

Date: 2026-04-08
Mode: Classic only

## Objective
Turn Classic into a stateful consequence game where choices reshape event weighting, path access, severity, recovery, and score pace.

## Systems That Must Matter
- Budget stance
- Wealth strategy
- Housing
- Transport
- Education
- Focus
- Career track
- Credit
- Family/social support

## System Influence Contract
- Budget stance
  - Upside: monthly margin control and debt pace
  - Downside: life pressure and burnout drag
  - Event hooks: debt/credit squeeze, burnout strain
  - Gating hooks: indirect via stability and credit
  - Recovery hooks: stronger with balanced/quality-of-life posture
  - Scoring pressure: debt ratio, surplus consistency
  - Long-run branch consequence: resilient vs brittle run profile
- Wealth strategy
  - Upside: higher long-run score ceiling, compounding
  - Downside: lower shock absorption when overexposed
  - Event hooks: market shock and debt refinancing windows
  - Gating hooks: cash access and debt pressure
  - Recovery hooks: liquidity-first routes when conservative
  - Scoring pressure: net worth and volatility drag
  - Long-run branch consequence: high-ceiling vs low-risk arc
- Housing
  - Upside: recovery quality, stability, stress relief
  - Downside: rent squeeze and instability cascades
  - Event hooks: rent/roommate/housing squeeze
  - Gating hooks: credit and support requirements
  - Recovery hooks: fallback and stabilization routes
  - Scoring pressure: housing stability category
  - Long-run branch consequence: stable base vs recurring shocks
- Transport
  - Upside: work access, consistency, income reliability
  - Downside: breakdown/repair and ownership drag
  - Event hooks: transport friction families
  - Gating hooks: career access and credit thresholds
  - Recovery hooks: downgrade/switch paths
  - Scoring pressure: stability via consistency
  - Long-run branch consequence: access lane vs friction lane
- Education
  - Upside: credential and branch unlocks
  - Downside: active stress load and monthly cash squeeze
  - Event hooks: education drag and scholarship windows
  - Gating hooks: career entry and promotion
  - Recovery hooks: intensity and pause/resume management
  - Scoring pressure: education breakdown category
  - Long-run branch consequence: higher ceiling with higher short-run load
- Focus
  - Upside: tactical monthly edge
  - Downside: immediate stress/energy tradeoffs
  - Event hooks: modifies monthly pressure families
  - Gating hooks: indirect via stress/energy and progress
  - Recovery hooks: recovery/social maintenance paths
  - Scoring pressure: momentum and burnout profile
  - Long-run branch consequence: snowball vs stabilization arc
- Career track
  - Upside: income/mobility and branch-specific upside
  - Downside: layoff/turbulence profile
  - Event hooks: career turbulence and breakthrough windows
  - Gating hooks: transport, education, momentum
  - Recovery hooks: lateral pivots and reset paths
  - Scoring pressure: career tier and stability
  - Long-run branch consequence: path identity and replay diversity
- Credit
  - Upside: unlocks housing/transport/refinance paths
  - Downside: fragility closes options and worsens terms
  - Event hooks: credit squeeze and refinance families
  - Gating hooks: hard unlock/blockers
  - Recovery hooks: payment consistency and debt normalization
  - Scoring pressure: debt ratio quality
  - Long-run branch consequence: expanding vs shrinking option surface
- Family/social support
  - Upside: recovery buffer and bailout routes
  - Downside: low support amplifies collapse risk
  - Event hooks: family pressure and support buffer windows
  - Gating hooks: fallback housing and lifeline access
  - Recovery hooks: support-dependent recovery routes
  - Scoring pressure: life stability and burnout resistance
  - Long-run branch consequence: recoverable vs brittle run states
